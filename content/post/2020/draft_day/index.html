Limeira Guara Wolf

Built Around a Terrific Bunch of WRs, Orange Boys is Poised for the Title

Orange Boys had success drafting in the third spot of the opening round, building a roster with playoff potential. They stocked up on pass-catching weapons early, using three of their first five selections to pick up wide receivers. Elsewhere on the roster, Orange Boys was able to land the highest-scoring combo of tight ends in the league.

Paulinia Robots

With a League Title Projected, Paulinia Robots Bets on the Running Game

Paulinia Robots should officially be on the Its Football Dudes radar after taking the fifth overall draft slot and delivering a team that may be playoff caliber. They grabbed running backs like they were going out of style, using their first three selections exclusively on ball-carriers. In conjunction with that drafting strategy, Paulinia Robots ended up with the highest-scoring foursome of running backs in the league.

Barao Butchers

Led by a Terrific Bevy of RBs, Barao Butchers Should be Poised for World Domination

Landing the No. 1 pick in the draft doesn’t always mean a championship. However in this case, it’s a real possibility. Barao Butchers converted their No. 1 overall selection into this year’s top draft. They jumped on ball-carriers early, using three of their first five picks to acquire running backs. That early positional investment helped to yield the most prolific trio of running backs in the league.

Amparo Bikers

Anchored by an Outstanding Set of Backs, Amparo Bikers Expects to Land Among the League’s Elite

Amparo Bikers parlayed a good draft slot into a respectable performance. They didn’t overload at any one position early on, instead focusing on balance by drafting a quarterback, running back, wide receiver, and tight end in their first four picks. As the draft concluded, Amparo Bikers secured one of the top foursomes of running backs in the league.

Indaiatuba Blues

Such was the case with Indaiatuba Blues, which flipped their 12th overall pick into a projected fourth-place finish in Its Football Dudes. They stocked up on pass-catching weapons early, using three of their first five selections to pick up wide receivers. Corresponding with that strategy, Indaiatuba Blues landed one of the most prolific quartets of wide receivers in the league.
