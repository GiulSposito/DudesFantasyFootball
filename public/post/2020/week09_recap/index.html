Matchups Recap

Behind a Sizzling Showing from Dalvin Cook, Paulinia Robots Beats Goncalves Miners

With the production from Dalvin Cook playing an integral role, Paulinia Robots defeated Goncalves Miners 144.54 to 117.60. Cook heated up for 39.20 points in a 26.94-point win for Paulinia Robots. He rushed for 206 yards as part of his huge game (206 Rsh Yds, 46 Rec Yds, 2 TD). He also recorded the highest point total in the league this week. With 144.54 points, Paulinia Robots had the highest total in the league this week. Paulinia Robots (5-4, 1,133.36 points) climbs into fifth place, while Goncalves Miners (3-6, 1,060.34 points) steps into 12th place.

James Conner had plenty of chances for Paulinia Robots, but he logged just 22 total yards on 11 touches. One spot where Goncalves Miners made a blunder is wide receiver. Tyreek Hill could have started in place of Adam Thielen, and that point difference would have changed the outcome.

Cook has now led Paulinia Robots in scoring in consecutive weeks.

In Week 10, Paulinia Robots plays Campinas Giants (6-3, 1,054.12), while Goncalves Miners gets Brotas Alligators (3-6, 974.64).

Sorocaba Steelers Beats Sorocaba Wild Mules

Jerry Jeudy racked up 25.50 points and Ben Roethlisberger added another 25.04 points with 306 passing yards as Sorocaba Steelers beat Sorocaba Wild Mules 123.14 to 107.50. Sorocaba Steelers beat expectations and put up 17.6% more points than projected (104.69). Sorocaba Wild Mules got just 85.8% of a projected 125.26 points. Sorocaba Wild Mules was led by Keenan Allen with 25.30 points (103 Rec Yds, 1 TD) and Russell Wilson, who scored 22.10 (390 Pas Yds, 2 Pas TD/2 Int, 1 Rsh TD). Sorocaba Wild Mules (6-3, 1,002.02 points) steps into first place and Sorocaba Steelers (4-5, 988.74 points) remains in 10th place.

While Julio Jones led Sorocaba Steelers with 20.70 points in Week 8, Jeudy paced the squad this week with 25.50.

In Week 10, Sorocaba Steelers takes on Amparo Bikers (4-5, 1,032.88), while Sorocaba Wild Mules gets London Knights (4-5, 1,002.64).

Amparo Bikers Slaps Indaiatuba Riders, Rises to 8th Place

Amparo Bikers picked up 30.88 points from Patrick Mahomes (372 Pas Yds, 4 TD), demolishing Indaiatuba Riders 106.48 to 70.84. This beatdown was revenge for a loss to Indaiatuba Riders in their last meeting a season ago. Chase Claypool (acquired via free agency in Week 6) made a big impact, posting 15.30 points. Amparo Bikers (4-5, 1,032.88 points) climbs into eighth place and Indaiatuba Riders (2-7, 962.88 points) is still looking up at everyone from last place.

Devin Singletary ultimately deserved more touches for Amparo Bikers, as he put up a whopping 34 total yards on just five touches.

Mahomes has now led Amparo Bikers in scoring in back-to-back weeks, and for three of the last five.

Amparo Bikers gets Sorocaba Steelers (4-5, 988.74) in Week 10, while Indaiatuba Riders matches up with Rio Claro Farmers (6-3, 1,011.98).
