League Highlights

Matchups Recap

Paulinia Robots Beats Amparo Bikers, Rises to 5th Place

Paulinia Robots beat Amparo Bikers 154.00 to 133.60 and got the highest score this season. A rally in prime time on Monday Night Football (40.00 MNF points, 30% of their final score) wasn’t enough for Amparo Bikers. Paulinia Robots took a 46.10-point lead on Sunday afternoon and maintained that advantage for the rest of the matchup. Amparo Bikers actually averages more per game in losses (124.02) than wins (123.18). Each side met their expectations, but Paulinia Robots put up 31.0% more points than projected (117.54) to earn the win. Paulinia Robots (2-1, 378.44 points) climbs into fifth place and Amparo Bikers (1-2, 371.22 points) drops to 10th place.

Elliott had plenty of opportunities for Amparo Bikers, but could only manage 58 total yards on 20 touches.

Next week, Paulinia Robots squares off against Sorocaba Steelers (2-1, 308.88), while Amparo Bikers goes up against Rio Claro Farmers (1-2, 331.58).

Sorocaba Wild Mules Picks Up Win Against Rio Claro Farmers to Capture 2nd Place

A rally on Sunday Night Football (41.12 points, 40% of their total score) wasn’t enough for Rio Claro Farmers, as Sorocaba Wild Mules got the 113.20 to 104.02 victory. The late-match efforts were somewhat unexpected, considering their original Sunday night projection was only 35.94 points. Sorocaba Wild Mules was led by Russell Wilson (315 Pas Yds, 1 2PT, 5 TD). Wilson passed for five touchdowns and provided 36.80 points to the final tally. It could have gone the other way had Rio Claro Farmers not had a starter score zero points (John Brown). Sorocaba Wild Mules (2-1, 317.70 points) climbs into second place, while Rio Claro Farmers (1-2, 331.58 points) drops to 12th place.

There was one starter on Rio Claro Farmers that failed to capitalize on opportunites. Despite being targeted eight times, Mark Andrews could manage just three catches. There are a few spots where Rio Claro Farmers really made a regrettable choice, unfortunately. Cooper Kupp could have started in place of either Brown or Russell Gage, while Sony Michel could’ve played over Leonard Fournette. Any of those point differentials would have changed the outcome.

Next week, Sorocaba Wild Mules plays Limeira Guara Wolf (1-2, 326.78), while Rio Claro Farmers faces Amparo Bikers (1-2, 371.22).

Sorocaba Steelers Squeaks By Indaiatuba Riders to Grab 8th Place

Sorocaba Steelers picked up 23.10 points from Mike Davis (46 Rsh Yds, 45 Rec Yds, 1 Rec TD) and 19.18 from Ben Roethlisberger (237 Pas Yds, 1 2PT, 2 TD) to sneak by Indaiatuba Riders 104.08 to 100.86. Sorocaba Steelers took a 2.22-point lead on Sunday afternoon and held the lead throughout the matchup. Sorocaba Steelers has been red-hot in this matchup as of late, winning three straight against Indaiatuba Riders. Sorocaba Steelers (2-1, 308.88 points) climbs into eighth place and Indaiatuba Riders (0-3, 334.28 points) stays in last place.

Montgomery had plenty of opportunities for Indaiatuba Riders, but could only manage 54 total yards on 16 touches.

Next week, Sorocaba Steelers faces Paulinia Robots (2-1, 378.44). Indaiatuba Riders takes on London Knights (2-1, 394.08), who has averaged an impressive 131.36 points this season.

London Knights Gets Late Victory over Barao Butchers, Takes Top Spot in League

London Knights (2-1, 394.08 points) defeated Barao Butchers (2-1, 403.72 points) 138.10 to 130.18 in a matchup with three lead changes. They also put up the second-highest score for the week. London Knights took the early lead, up 103.50 to 60.30, after they scored the bulk of their points during the first set of Sunday games. On the heels of an outstanding performance during late Sunday games by Barao Butchers, they lost that lead and fell behind 130.18 to 111.70. However, London Knights recovered for the win on Monday night, with Clyde Edwards-Helaire scoring 18.40 points during the Chiefs/Ravens game.

There was one player on London Knights that failed to capitalize on opportunites. Even though Calvin Ridley saw a total of 13 targets, he reeled in just five catches. A spot where Barao Butchers is really kicking themselves is WR. Allen Lazard could have started in place of Diontae Johnson, and that point difference would’ve ultimately changed the outcome.

Next week, London Knights takes on Indaiatuba Riders (0-3, 334.28), while Barao Butchers matches up with Indaiatuba Blues (2-1, 344.94).

Limeira Dead Rabbits Beats Limeira Guara Wolf, Drops Them into 13th Place

Limeira Guara Wolf labored to score points this week and fell to Limeira Dead Rabbits 99.88 to 91.70. While each team failed to hit their projections, Limeira Dead Rabbits scored 98.1% of their projected 101.79 points to get the win. Tyler Lockett racked up the third-highest score of the league this week with 37.00 points (100 Rec Yds, 3 TD) for Limeira Guara Wolf. With Davante Adams delivering zero points for Limeira Dead Rabbits, the result might have been even worse. Limeira Guara Wolf (1-2, 326.78 points) drops to 13th place and Limeira Dead Rabbits (2-1, 355.26 points) remains in sixth place.

On Limeira Dead Rabbits, the New Orleans Saints DEF wasn’t just the one DEF to post a negative score; they represented the only fantasy starter throughout the entire league to do so. Chase Edmonds probably deserved more touches for Limeira Guara Wolf, as he put up an efficient 34 all-purpose yards on just five total touches. A position where Limeira Guara Wolf really made a painful call is QB. Josh Allen could have started in place of Gardner Minshew, and that point difference would’ve changed the outcome.

Limeira Dead Rabbits takes on Goncalves Miners (1-2, 377.44) in Week 4, while Limeira Guara Wolf faces Sorocaba Wild Mules (2-1, 317.70).

Behind a Sizzling Showing from Austin Ekeler, Brotas Alligators Gets Win over Indaiatuba Blues

With the contribution from Austin Ekeler playing an integral role, Brotas Alligators beat Indaiatuba Blues 105.60 to 86.82. Ekeler blew up for 31.30 points in a 18.78-point win for Brotas Alligators. Along with the Los Angeles Chargers RB (59 Rsh Yds, 84 Rec Yds, 1 TD), Kyler Murray (270 Pas Yds, 29 Rsh Yds, 2 Pas TD/3 Int, 1 Rsh TD) pitched in with 21.70 points. Brotas Alligators (1-2, 341.10 points) climbs into 11th place, while Indaiatuba Blues (2-1, 344.94 points) drops to seventh place.

Joe Mixon and Melvin Gordon had plenty of chances for Brotas Alligators, but they logged just 103 total yards on 31 combined touches between them.

Brotas Alligators matches up against Campinas Giants (2-1, 391.50) in Week 4. Indaiatuba Blues gets Barao Butchers (2-1, 403.72), who is averaging a league-leading 134.57 points per game.

Campinas Giants Pulls Off Comeback over Goncalves Miners, Moves Up to 4th Place

Campinas Giants was clutch under the lights with 69.72 points on Sunday Night Football (51% of their total score) to get the comeback victory over Goncalves Miners 135.42 to 107.88. The late heroics were somewhat unexpected, since they were originally projected to score 51.79 points on Sunday night. Campinas Giants was led by Alvin Kamara (58 Rsh Yds, 139 Rec Yds, 2 Rec TD). Kamara scored two touchdowns and provided 44.70 points to the total. Campinas Giants (2-1, 391.50 points) climbs into fourth place, while Goncalves Miners (1-2, 377.44 points) drops to ninth place.

There was one player on Campinas Giants that missed out on opportunities. Despite being targeted seven times, Darius Slayton could manage just three catches.

Campinas Giants plays Brotas Alligators (1-2, 341.10) in Week 4, while Goncalves Miners matches up against Limeira Dead Rabbits (2-1, 355.26).
